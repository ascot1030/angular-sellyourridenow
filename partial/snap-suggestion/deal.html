▼ Overview

You want to gain your first yes from your prospect by offering them a simple "deal". The deal reinforces the promises concerning the content you'll be delivering, and the benefits they'll reap from this content, and then opens a loop to begin Step 2: The Vital Connection. A 'loop' is an NLP (Neurolinguistic Programming) term that simply means you hint at a topic you'll cover, say you'll come back to it later. You close the loop when you do come back to it later. This keeps the mind open and in a state of anticipation.
