▼ Overview

It's time for product/service graphics and specifics about what your product/service offers. Here, feel free to brag about the unique features and selling points of the product/service specifically. Note: you can drop the buy button at this point if you like. (See the tutorial video "The Magic Buy Button" in the “Producing Your VSL” section.)

Note: After you first series of slides, continue in the same exact pattern (coined phrases are optional) of "on page/day X", what's on that page/day, and then the details on the following slide, for as many product/service specifics as you desire. If you are in a service business, you can simply let your viewers know what to expect when you come into your store or engage your services.
